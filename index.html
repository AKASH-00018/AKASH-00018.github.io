Hands-on Cybersecurity Labs and Projects

As part of my cybersecurity learning journey, I explored several labs and tools to understand how vulnerabilities are discovered and mitigated. Here are some highlights:

  • Network Scanning with Nmap: Conducted host discovery and service enumeration to identify open ports and services.
  • Traffic Analysis with Wireshark: Captured live packets, studied TCP/IP communication, and spotted suspicious traffic.
  • Web Application Testing with Burp Suite: Intercepted HTTP requests, tested for SQLi & XSS in labs.
  • Access Control Vulnerabilities: Explored IDOR and broken access control issues, showing how attackers bypass restrictions.
  • Business Logic Flaws: Identified weaknesses in workflows like bypassing payments or exploiting discounts.
  • Metasploit Framework: Practiced basic exploitation on vulnerable machines (Metasploitable VM).

These labs strengthened my understanding of penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, and secure coding awareness. More write-ups and projects are available on my GitHub.
